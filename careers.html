Naval Officer

Description: Lead your crew and engage in vital naval operations. A challenging but rewarding career.

Requirements

  • Must be 18 or older
  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Willingness to relocate

Responsibilities

  • Supervising crew members
  • Coordinating naval operations
  • Ensuring the safety of the vessel and crew

Engineer

Description: Work on maintaining and improving the Royal Navy's fleet. A key role in ensuring operational readiness.

Requirements

  • Degree in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Ability to work under pressure

Responsibilities

  • Performing maintenance on naval equipment
  • Repairing faulty machinery
  • Collaborating with other departments to improve fleet performance

Medic

Description: Provide crucial medical support to the crew during missions and training exercises.

Requirements

  • Medical degree or equivalent qualifications
  • Ability to remain calm under pressure
  • First Aid and CPR certification

Responsibilities

  • Providing medical care to injured crew members
  • Maintaining medical equipment
  • Assisting with health assessments

Logistics Officer

Description: Manage and coordinate all logistics operations to ensure that the fleet operates smoothly.

Requirements

  • Experience in logistics or supply chain management
  • Organizational and planning skills
  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment

Responsibilities

  • Coordinating supply deliveries to vessels
  • Managing inventory and supplies
  • Ensuring operational efficiency in logistics

IT Specialist

Description: Manage and support the Navy’s IT infrastructure, ensuring systems are running smoothly and securely.

Requirements

  • Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Experience with networking and cybersecurity
  • Ability to troubleshoot and resolve technical issues

Responsibilities

  • Maintaining IT systems and networks
  • Ensuring data security protocols are followed
  • Providing technical support to staff

Public Relations Officer

Description: Manage and improve the Navy’s public image and media presence.

Requirements

  • Degree in Public Relations, Communications, or Marketing
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience in media relations

Responsibilities

  • Creating and distributing press releases
  • Managing the Navy’s social media accounts
  • Organizing public events and communications
